Manager App

What is Manager App?

Manager App is a web application whose purpose is to help our clients in developing their business. We do this by providing a fast modular platform on which our customers can place different shipments they need to get or deliver. Later every shipment will have a vehicle assigned by the administrator of the transport company.

Why chose Manager App?

There are multiple reasons in choosing this app.

  • fast platform
  • big fleet of vehicles staying at your disposition
  • prompt administrators regarding your shipments
  • friendly and intuitive interface
  • possibility to change or cancel shipments at any time
  • continuous development without creating interruptions
